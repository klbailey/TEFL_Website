About

I’m Katherine Bailey Hines, a certified TEFL instructor with specialties in Business English and Teaching English Online. I’m passionate about helping adult learners improve their professional English skills. With a background in technology and insurance, I’m eager to combine these skills to create engaging lessons. Teaching Philosophy

I believe in creating tailored, student-centered lesson plans that reflect each learner’s unique goals and interests. By connecting lessons to topics my students care about, I help them develop practical language skills and confidence for real-world communication.
